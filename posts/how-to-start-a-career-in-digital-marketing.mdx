Digital marketing is one of the most accessible fields in the tech world. To start:

1. **Learn the basics**: SEO, PPC, content marketing, and email marketing.  
2. **Start a blog** or personal website to experiment and grow your digital footprint.  
3. **Get certified**: Google Digital Garage, HubSpot Academy, or Meta Blueprint are great starting points.  
4. **Work on small projects** for friends, family, or local businesses to build a portfolio.  

A formal degree is optional, but curiosity, analytical thinking, and creativity are must-haves.
